Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for the issues we see most often.

📷

Camera permission denied

Open your device Settings → FridgeChef and enable Camera access. Then relaunch the app and try scanning again.

📶

Internet connection

FridgeChef needs an active connection to analyze photos and generate recipes. Switch between Wi‑Fi and cellular to test.

⚡

Slow AI analysis

Heavy traffic or a weak signal can slow things down. Use a well‑lit photo and a stable network for the fastest results.

🍳

Recipe generation failed

Try again in a few seconds — our servers may be busy. If it persists, reduce the number of ingredients or restart the app.

🥕

No ingredients detected

Take the photo in bright, even light with items clearly visible. Avoid clutter and packaging for the best recognition.

🔄

App not updating

Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store. Updates roll out frequently with improved AI and new recipes.

🔒 Privacy first

Your photos stay yours.

  • Photos are only used temporarily to analyze the ingredients in your fridge.
  • Photos are not permanently stored by FridgeChef on our servers.
  • The analysis is performed securely over encrypted connections.
